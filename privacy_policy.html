1. Introduction

Welcome to CollectScan ("App," "we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience with our service. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

Key Privacy Principles:
  • We collect only necessary information to provide our service
  • We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties
  • You have full control over your account and data
  • We use industry-standard encryption and security measures

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Registration: Email address, password (encrypted), and optional profile information when you create an account. You may also use the app as a guest via anonymous authentication.
  • Card Collection Data: Information about cards you scan or manually add (card names, editions, conditions, prices, notes, deck configurations).
  • Marketplace Listings: Photos, descriptions, prices, condition, category, and location (city/state) you provide when creating marketplace listings.
  • Restock Alert Preferences: Your selected retailers (Target, Walmart, Amazon, Best Buy, GameStop) and product categories for restock notifications.
  • Payment Information: Subscription details processed securely through Google Play Billing (we do not store payment card data).

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Camera Access: Your device's camera is used only when you initiate card scanning. Images are processed on-device using ML Kit text recognition. We do not store camera images unless you explicitly save them.
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system, app version, unique device identifiers for analytics purposes.
  • Usage Analytics: How you interact with the app (features used, pages visited, actions performed) through Firebase Analytics.
  • Crash Reports: Automatic error and crash data to help us improve stability.
  • Location Data: We do not collect precise location data. General location may be inferred from IP address for regional analytics only.

2.3 Information from Third Parties

We receive card pricing data and market information from eBay APIs to provide real-time price updates. We also aggregate publicly available restock information from major retailers to power our restock alert features.

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Firebase Services (Google)

  • Firebase Authentication: Secure login, anonymous authentication, and account management
  • Firebase Firestore: Cloud database for storing your collection data, securely encrypted
  • Firebase Storage: Secure storage for card images and marketplace listing photos
  • Firebase Analytics: Aggregate usage analytics to understand feature usage and improve the app

Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4.2 Google AdMob

We display advertisements to free-tier users through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. You can upgrade to VIP ($4.99/month or $39.99/year) to remove all ads.

Google AdMob Privacy: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

4.3 eBay API

We use eBay's Browse API to retrieve real-time market pricing for scanned cards. Only card names and identifiers are sent to eBay; no personal data is shared through this integration.

4.4 Google ML Kit

Card scanning uses Google ML Kit for on-device text recognition. All image processing occurs locally on your device. No card images are sent to Google or our servers during scanning.

4.5 Google Play Billing

VIP subscription billing is processed through Google Play Billing. We do not store your payment card information. Google handles all financial data securely.

Important: These third parties have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them as we are not responsible for their practices. We only share the minimum necessary information to provide our services.

5. Data Security

  • All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses SSL/TLS encryption (HTTPS)
  • Sensitive data is encrypted at rest in Firebase
  • Firestore Security Rules restrict data access to authorized users only
  • Authentication credentials are managed securely by Firebase Auth
Security Reminder: While we implement strong security measures, no online system is 100% secure. Please protect your account password and report any suspicious activity immediately.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Update Your Data

You can access, review, and update your profile information and collection data anytime through the app's Settings screen.

6.2 Delete Your Account

You can request account deletion by contacting us. When you delete your account, your profile and personal information are deleted immediately, and your collection and marketplace data are deleted within 30 days.

6.3 Manage Notifications

You can manage push notification and restock alert preferences in your device settings and within the App's settings.

6.4 Opt-Out of Advertisements

Free-tier users can subscribe to VIP to remove all advertisements. You can also manage ad personalization preferences in your Google Account settings.

6.5 California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information is collected, request deletion, and opt-out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

7. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

COPPA Compliance: CollectScan is not directed to children under 13 years old, and we comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has created an account, we will promptly delete the account and associated data.

If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information to us, please contact us immediately at jkjg1990@gmail.com.
