We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience with our service. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App. Key Privacy Principles: We collect only necessary information to provide our service We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties You have full control over your account and data We use industry-standard encryption and security measures 2. Information We Collect 2.1 Information You Provide Directly Account Registration: Email address, password (encrypted), and optional profile information when you create an account Card Collection Data: Information about cards you scan or manually add (card names, editions, conditions, prices, notes) Listing Information: Details when you create eBay or TCGplayer listings (card details, pricing, descriptions) Payment Information: Subscription details processed securely through Google Play Billing (we do not store payment card data) Customer Support: Messages, inquiries, and feedback you send to our support team 2.2 Information Collected Automatically Camera Access: Your device's camera is used only when you initiate card scanning. We do not store images unless you explicitly save them in your collection Device Information: Device model, operating system, app version, unique device identifiers for analytics purposes Usage Analytics: How you interact with the app (features used, pages visited, actions performed) through Firebase Analytics Crash Reports: Automatic error and crash data to help us improve stability Location Data: We do not collect precise location data. General location may be inferred from IP address for regional analytics only 2.3 Information from Third Parties We may receive card pricing data and market information from eBay and TCGplayer APIs to provide real-time price updates to you. 3. How We Use Your Information We use the information we collect for the following purposes: Purpose Information Used Legal Basis Provide core app functionality Account, collection, scanning data Contract performance Authentication and security Email, password, device ID Contract & legitimate interest Process subscriptions Payment information (via Google Play) Contract performance Improve app and services Usage analytics, crash reports Legitimate business interest Customer support Your inquiries and account information Contract & customer service Send important notifications Email address, device ID Contract obligation Display advertisements (Free tier) Device ID, usage patterns, demographics Legitimate interest We will never use your information to: Sell or rent your personal data to third parties Send unsolicited marketing emails (you can opt out anytime) Share your collection details without explicit permission Use your card collection data for competitor advantage 4. Third-Party Services We use the following third-party services to operate MyVault: 4.1 Firebase Services (Google) Firebase Authentication: Secure login and account management Firebase Firestore: Cloud database for storing your collection data, securely encrypted Firebase Storage: Secure storage for card images and backup data Firebase Analytics: Aggregate usage analytics to understand feature usage and improve the app Firebase Crashlytics: Automatic crash reporting to help us fix bugs Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy 4.2 Google AdMob We display advertisements to free-tier users through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data to serve relevant ads. Advertisers do not receive your personal information. You can opt to subscribe to VIP to remove all ads. Google AdMob Privacy: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543 4.3 eBay and TCGplayer APIs When you list cards or check prices, we connect to eBay and TCGplayer APIs to: Retrieve real-time market pricing Create listings on your behalf (with your authorization) Track your sales performance Your eBay/TCGplayer account data is NOT shared with us. You authorize API access through your own accounts directly. eBay Privacy: https://www.ebay.com/help/policies/account/privacy-center TCGplayer Privacy: https://www.tcgplayer.com/privacy-policy 4.4 Google Play Services Subscription billing is processed through Google Play Billing. We do not store your payment card information. Google handles all financial data securely. Important: These third parties have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them as we are not responsible for their practices. We only share the minimum necessary information to provide our services. 5. Data Security We take data security seriously and implement industry-standard measures to protect your information: 5.1 Encryption All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses SSL/TLS encryption (HTTPS) Sensitive data (passwords, payment info) is encrypted at rest in Firebase Database records are encrypted with AES-256 encryption 5.2 Access Controls Only authorized staff can access your data in Firebase Access is logged and monitored for suspicious activity Authentication requires multi-factor identification for admin accounts 5.3 Regular Security Audits We conduct security assessments regularly Third-party security firms periodically audit our infrastructure We monitor for vulnerabilities and patch immediately Security Reminder: While we implement strong security measures, no online system is 100% secure. Please protect your account password and report any suspicious activity immediately to support@myvault.app 6. Your Rights and Choices 6.1 Access Your Data You can access, review, and export your collection data anytime through the app settings. We provide options to download your data in CSV or JSON format. 6.2 Update or Correct Your Information You can update your profile information, collection details, and preferences directly in the app at any time. 6.3 Delete Your Account and Data You have the right to delete your account and all associated data at any time. When you request account deletion: Your profile and personal information are deleted immediately Your collection data is securely deleted from our servers within 30 days Backup copies are deleted within 60 days Some data may be retained for legal compliance (e.g., transaction records for 7 years) To delete your account, go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. This action cannot be undone. 6.4 Opt-Out of Analytics You can disable analytics tracking in Settings > Privacy. Note that we still collect minimal crash data to fix critical bugs. 6.5 Opt-Out of Advertisements Free-tier users can subscribe to VIP to remove all advertisements. You can also manage ad preferences in your Google Account settings. 6.6 Opt-Out of Marketing Emails You can unsubscribe from marketing emails by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in any promotional email or through your account settings. 6.7 California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) If you are a California resident, you have additional rights including the right to know, delete, and opt-out of selling of personal information. Contact us at support@myvault.app to exercise these rights. 6.8 EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) If you are in the EU, you have rights to access, rectify, erase, and port your data. We will respond to these requests within 30 days as required by law. 7. Children's Privacy (COPPA) COPPA Compliance: MyVault is not directed to children under 13 years old, and we comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). 7.1 No Intentional Collection from Children We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has created an account: We will promptly delete the account and associated data We will notify the parent/guardian of the deletion No marketing or data sharing will occur 7.2 Parental Consent If your child is between 13-18 years old and wants to use MyVault, we recommend: A parent or guardian creates and manages the account Parental supervision of card collection and marketplace activities Review of this privacy policy together 7.3 Safe for Families MyVault features family-friendly design: No user-generated content moderation needed (app-only collection) No social chat or messaging features with strangers Ad content is family-appropriate Card games content is age-appropriate for teens and adults If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information, please contact us immediately at support@myvault.app 8.
